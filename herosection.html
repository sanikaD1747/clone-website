Many of the people I work with are thoughtful, successful, and self-aware—yet internally, they feel stuck in overthinking, emotionally on edge, or always bracing for the next problem.

My Specialties

Anxiety & Panic

For those who feel constantly wound up or unable to switch off. We work on regulating your nervous system and quieting the internal noise.

Trauma & EMDR

Healing from past experiences that linger in the present. My approach uses EMDR and body-oriented techniques to foster deep safety and recovery.

Burnout & Perfectionism

Reconnect with your creativity and joy. We address the high internal pressure that leads to professional and emotional exhaustion.
